MATERIALS, CARE AND ORIGIN


JOIN LIFE

Care for fibre: at least 50% recycled wool & 30% recycled polyamide


We use the Join Life label on clothing that is produced using technology and raw materials that help us to reduce environmental impact of our products.


RECYCLED WOOL

Recycled wool is produced from wool recovered from other garments or the fibre waste from other products.


This recovered wool is recycled through conventional recycling processes, i.e. the wool is sorted by type and colour and shredded. In order to guarantee the quality of the recycled thread, the shredded fibre is mixed with virgin fibre.


By transforming this waste into a new resource, we reduce the consumption of virgin raw material and, in turn, the consumption of water, energy and natural resources.
